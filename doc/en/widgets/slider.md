# Slider

[Back to README](../../../README.md#widget-documentation)

A horizontal or vertical native VIS 2 slider that reads and writes a numeric
state. Template id: `tplVis2-materialdesign-Slider`.

<img src="../../media/vis2_slider_runtime.png" alt="Material Design slider in VIS 2">

## Editor settings

<table>
<tr><td><img src="../../media/vis2_slider_editor_overview.png" width="300"></td>
<td><ul><li><b>oid:</b> value state; <b>oid-working</b> optionally reports interaction.</li><li>Set orientation, min, max and step in <b>Common</b>.</li><li><b>Scale:</b> ticks and tick labels.</li><li><b>Label:</b> raw value or percent, unit and limit texts.</li><li><b>Thumb label:</b> hidden, while dragging or always visible.</li></ul></td></tr>
</table>

Enable **read only** to display a value without writing it.
